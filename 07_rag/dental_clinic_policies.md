## 1. About BrightSmile Dental Clinic

BrightSmile Dental Clinic is a family-owned dental practice established in 2008. We provide general, cosmetic, and specialist dental care to patients of all ages. Our mission is to deliver gentle, modern, and affordable dentistry in a comfortable environment. The clinic is staffed by 6 licensed dentists, 2 dental hygienists, and a team of qualified support staff. All of our clinicians are registered with the national dental board and participate in ongoing professional development.

## 4. Booking an Appointment

Appointments can be booked online through our website, by phone, or in person at either branch reception. When booking, please have your patient ID ready if you are an existing patient. New patients will be asked to provide basic contact details and insurance information.

Standard check-up appointments last 30 minutes. Hygiene cleanings last 45 minutes. Longer procedures such as root canals or crown fittings are scheduled for 60 to 90 minutes. We recommend booking routine check-ups at least two weeks in advance, as availability fills quickly. Online booking is available 24 hours a day, but same-day appointments must be arranged by phone.

## 5. Cancellation and No-Show Policy

We require at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice are subject to a late cancellation fee of $40.

Patients who fail to attend an appointment without any notice ("no-show") will be charged a no-show fee of $60. After three no-shows within a 12-month period, the clinic reserves the right to require prepayment for all future appointments. Cancellation and no-show fees must be settled before the next appointment can be booked. These fees are not covered by insurance.

## 6. New Patient Information

New patients should arrive 15 minutes early for their first appointment to complete a medical and dental history form. Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card or details, and a list of any current medications. The first visit typically includes a comprehensive oral examination, diagnostic X-rays if needed, and a discussion of any recommended treatment.

The standard new patient examination fee is $90, which includes the consultation and basic X-rays. Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian who can provide consent for treatment.

## 7. Services Offered

BrightSmile provides a full range of dental services:

- **General dentistry:** check-ups, fillings, extractions, and preventive care.
- **Hygiene services:** professional cleaning, scaling, and polishing.
- **Cosmetic dentistry:** teeth whitening, veneers, and cosmetic bonding.
- **Restorative dentistry:** crowns, bridges, dentures, and dental implants.
- **Endodontics:** root canal treatment.
- **Orthodontics:** traditional braces and clear aligners (Downtown branch only).
- **Oral surgery:** wisdom tooth removal and minor surgical procedures (Downtown branch only).
- **Pediatric dentistry:** dental care tailored for children.

Specialist services may require a referral or an initial consultation before treatment begins.

## 9. Payment Methods and Billing

We accept cash, all major credit and debit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due at the time of treatment unless a payment plan has been agreed in advance.

For treatments over $1,000, we offer interest-free payment plans spread across 3, 6, or 12 months, subject to approval. A deposit of 20% is required to start a payment plan. Itemized receipts are provided for every transaction and can be emailed on request. Outstanding balances unpaid after 30 days may incur a late payment charge of 1.5% per month.

## 10. Insurance and Claims

BrightSmile works with most major dental insurance providers. We can process direct claims on your behalf for participating insurers, meaning you only pay the portion not covered by your plan. For non-participating insurers, you pay upfront and we provide the documentation needed to claim reimbursement yourself.

It is the patient's responsibility to confirm their coverage details and any pre-authorization requirements before treatment. Insurance does not cover missed appointment fees, late cancellation fees, or purely cosmetic procedures unless specified in your policy. Please bring your up-to-date insurance information to every appointment.

## 11. Membership and Savings Plan

For patients without insurance, BrightSmile offers an annual membership plan called the BrightSmile Care Plan. The plan costs $250 per year and includes two routine check-ups, two hygiene cleanings, and any necessary diagnostic X-rays at no additional charge.

Members also receive a 15% discount on all other treatments, including fillings, crowns, and cosmetic work. The membership is for individuals; a family plan covering up to four members is available for $700 per year. Membership fees are non-refundable and renew annually. The discount cannot be combined with insurance coverage on the same treatment.

## 12. Emergency and After-Hours Care

Dental emergencies include severe toothache, knocked-out teeth, significant swelling, uncontrolled bleeding, or trauma to the mouth. During opening hours, call us immediately and we will prioritize emergency cases, aiming to see you the same day.

Outside opening hours, call the general phone line to reach the on-call dentist for urgent advice. Emergency appointments carry an emergency assessment fee of $120 in addition to the cost of any treatment provided. If you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, such as difficulty breathing or uncontrollable bleeding, call emergency services or go to your nearest hospital immediately.

## 13. Children and Family Dentistry

We welcome patients of all ages and recommend a child's first dental visit by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears. Children's check-ups are offered at a reduced rate of $35 for patients under 12. Our pediatric team is trained to make visits friendly and stress-free.

Routine check-ups for children are recommended every six months. A parent or legal guardian must accompany any patient under 18 and provide consent for treatment. The BrightSmile family membership plan offers an affordable way to cover dental care for the whole household.

## 14. Sedation and Anxiety Management

We understand that many patients feel anxious about dental treatment. BrightSmile offers several options to help you stay comfortable, including topical numbing, local anesthetic, and inhalation sedation (laughing gas). For more complex cases, intravenous (IV) sedation is available at the Downtown branch by prior arrangement.

Sedation options carry additional fees: inhalation sedation is $90 per session and IV sedation starts at $300. If you choose IV sedation, you must arrange for a responsible adult to accompany you home, and you should not drive for 24 hours afterward. Please inform us in advance if you would like to discuss sedation options.

## 15. Privacy and Data Protection

BrightSmile is committed to protecting your personal and medical information. We collect and store patient data solely for the purpose of providing dental care, processing payments, and meeting legal obligations. Your records are kept confidential and are never sold to third parties.

We may share necessary information with your insurance provider to process claims, or with other healthcare professionals involved in your care, but only with your consent where required. Patients have the right to request a copy of their records and to ask for corrections to inaccurate information. Data is retained for the period required by law and then securely destroyed.

## 16. Complaints and Feedback

We aim to provide excellent care, but if something falls short, we want to know. Complaints can be made in person, by phone, or by email to feedback@brightsmiledental.example. All complaints are acknowledged within 2 working days and we aim to provide a full response within 14 working days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may escalate the matter to the national dental complaints service. We value all feedback, positive or negative, as it helps us improve our service. There is no charge for making a complaint and doing so will not affect the quality of your future care.

## 17. Accessibility and Special Assistance

Both branches are wheelchair accessible, with step-free entry and accessible restrooms. The Downtown branch has a ground-floor treatment room available for patients with mobility needs, and reserved accessible parking is available directly outside.

If you require assistance, such as a sign language interpreter, large-print documents, or extra time for your appointment, please let us know when booking so we can prepare. We are happy to accommodate assistance animals. Patients with specific medical or sensory needs are encouraged to discuss them with our reception team in advance.

## 18. Sterilization and Safety Standards

Patient safety is our highest priority. All reusable instruments are cleaned, disinfected, and sterilized in a medical-grade autoclave after every use, following national infection-control standards. Single-use items are disposed of safely after one patient.

Our treatment rooms are cleaned and disinfected between every appointment. All clinical staff follow strict hand-hygiene and personal protective equipment protocols. Our sterilization equipment is tested and certified regularly, and records are kept for inspection. If you have any concerns about hygiene or safety, our team is happy to explain our procedures.

## 19. Refund Policy

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. If a treatment has not yet been carried out, any deposit or prepayment is refundable minus a small administrative fee of $25. Once a treatment has begun, refunds are generally not available, though we will always work to resolve any concerns about the quality of care.

Membership fees and missed appointment fees are non-refundable. For custom items such as dentures, crowns, or aligners that have already been manufactured, the laboratory cost is non-refundable even if the treatment is not completed. Any approved refund is issued using the original payment method within 14 working days.

## 20. Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a referral to book an appointment?**
No. New and existing patients can book directly for general and cosmetic services. Some specialist treatments may require an initial consultation first.

**How often should I have a check-up?**
We recommend a routine check-up every six months for most patients, though your dentist may suggest a different schedule based on your needs.

**Can I be seen on the same day for a dental emergency?**
Yes. We prioritize dental emergencies and aim to see urgent cases the same day during opening hours. An emergency assessment fee applies.

**Do you treat nervous patients?**
Absolutely. We offer a range of comfort and sedation options and our team is experienced in helping anxious patients feel at ease.

**What happens if I am running late for my appointment?**
Please call us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate you, but if you arrive too late we may need to reschedule, and a late cancellation fee could apply.

**Do you accept walk-ins?**
We strongly recommend booking in advance. Walk-ins are accepted only if same-day availability allows, and emergencies are always prioritized.
